We manage Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter) as standard. TikTok and YouTube Shorts are available on Growth Social and above. We recommend platforms based on where your actual customers spend time — not just which platforms are trending — so you're not wasting effort on channels that don't fit your audience.

Ad spend budget is separate and managed transparently: you see exactly where every pound is going. For dedicated paid social campaigns, this is also available as a standalone add-on.

Every plan includes a monthly performance report covering: reach and impressions, engagement rate by post type, follower growth, link clicks and website referral traffic, and — where paid posts are used — cost per engagement and estimated enquiry attribution. Reports are in plain English, not just raw numbers.
