What's included

Everything a business-critical website needs

Mobile-first responsive design

Every page is designed to perform perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. Tested across screens before handoff.

Clear, performance-ready structure

Every section is written to move visitors toward one action: a call, form, enquiry, or booking. No wasted space.

On-page SEO setup

Semantic HTML, meta tags, heading hierarchy, schema markup, and page speed optimization built into every build.

No platform lock-in

Clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You own it. No subscription required, no plugin maintenance headaches.

Analytics and event tracking

Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, and custom CTA tracking set up so you know what's working from day one.

30-day post-launch support

After launch we review your analytics, fix any issues, and make copy or CTA adjustments based on real user data.

Cloudflare protection and SSL — included

Your site is deployed behind Cloudflare from day one. SSL is provisioned and auto-renewed, DDoS protection runs at the network edge, and DNS is managed securely — all at no extra cost.

DKIM, DMARC, and SPF — email authentication

Your domain's email authentication records are configured before handoff. Prevents spoofing, improves inbox deliverability, and satisfies Google and Microsoft sender requirements from launch.
